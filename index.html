ABOUT

Yummi Gummi is a collection of 4000 chewy bears living across omni-chains in Web 3.0., by artist & founder Esme. The project vision is to create an art-centric community that incubates high potential artists and projects that bring long-term value to its holders. Yummi Gummi encapsulates the most loved bears that resonate across cultures, backgrounds and experiences. Who doesn’t love a yummi gummi?

Yummi Roadmap

We will continue to create aesthetic art and work on developing and growing the opportunity set for our holders. Through Web 2 & Web 3 partnerships in art and the financial services sector, we will be able to provide unique project opportunities for our holders that can bring long-term value to the community.

Holders of Yummi Gummi will have a fun & cute NFT, along with access to a community of like-minded holders from around the world. NFT stands for “non-fungible token” which are unique digital items that are stored and encrypted on the blockchain that people can buy, own, and trade. NFTs can simply be digital art but can have various benefits. They can be treated like membership cards where holders get exclusive access to things like websites, events, merchandise, and more.

About the founder

Hi! My name is Resa and I am the founder & artist behind Yummi Gummi. I am a freelance artist and financial analyst with more than 6 years of experience in financial services across trading, investment banking and credit investing. Art is my passion and utility is an important life motto, my art work has always aimed to express an artistic attention to detail whilst bringing joy and positivity to its onlookers. I also believe that there are many ways to create value for a community and hope to bridge my Wall Street experience to Web 3.0 and bring unique investment opportunities to the Yummi Gummi community. I have ambitious dreams for Yummi Gummies and hope that you enjoy the art and are in with us on our journey ahead.
